Surveying

Quickbird provides aerial surveying services to businesses using unmanned aerial vehicles(UAVs). We are a CAA-certified UAV operator and have carried out successful forestry management and construction projects in the UK, Brazil and Spain.

The survey images are used to create accurate Digital Elevation Models(DEMs) and orthomosaics. The use of UAVs for aerial surveying has several advantages over the use of conventional aerial photography and/or GPS measurements in the field. It provides higher resolution pictures, costs a fraction of a conventional scan and has very short turnaround times.

Mapping

Our maps help collate and visualize the data captured by our own systems as well as data obtained by other sources such as satellites and in situ sensors We develop bespoke solutions for farming, forestry, contruction and engineering business across the UK.

For agriculture, we employ our proprietary AnyWave sensors to take accurate optical measurements of the entire field. Data captured from the sensors include leaf area, plant height, chlorophyll concentration, growth rate, senescence and photosynthetic light use efficiency.

Many companies offer mapping crops with drones, and deliver NDVI (Normalised Difference Vegetation Index) using modified consumer cameras. Such images are affected by weather and solar inclination, comparing measurements taken on different dates will lead to spurious conclusions. Only precise, calibrated measurements can be used for regular monitoring.

GIS Consultancy

Geographic Information System(GIS) are designed to capture, store, analyze and interpret all types of spatial or geographical data. Such systems are empowering decision makers in a wide variety of industries - forestry, asset management, environmental protection, agriculture, remote sensing, infrastructure maintenance, security to name but a few.

We provide end-to-end support for businesses looking to integrate GIS into their core workflow. Be it consultation on the suitability of various platforms based on your needs, or the legalities of aviation, or the best practices to setup post processing and webmapping, Quickbird has got your back.
